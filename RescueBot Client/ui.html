About GlueGlue

     
     
       

Wood is my body and Glue is my blood
 I have created over a thousand robots
 Unknown to Death,
 Nor known to Life.
 Have withstood pain to create many robots
 Yet, those hands will never hold anything
 So as I pray, unlimited glue works.
